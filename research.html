Best AI Tools for Research

Perplexity AI

Perplexity AI is an advanced research assistant that provides accurate answers with internet sources and references. It helps users collect information quickly for assignments, projects, and study purposes.

Consensus AI

Consensus AI focuses on scientific research and academic information. It helps students search research papers, journals, and evidence-based answers easily.

Scite AI

Scite AI analyzes scientific papers and shows whether research articles support or contradict certain findings. It is useful for academic and technical research.

Elicit

Elicit is an AI research assistant that automates literature reviews and helps researchers summarize complex papers and extract important insights quickly.

ChatGPT

ChatGPT helps users understand difficult topics, generate summaries, prepare notes, and explain concepts in simple language for better learning and productivity.
